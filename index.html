Interactive Accessibility Demo

Experience and understand various visual impairments through our interactive simulations. Test how different accessibility features can improve user experience.

Text-to-Speech

Convert any text into spoken words. Adjust speech rate and volume to suit your preferences, making written content accessible to those with visual impairments or reading difficulties.

Color Contrast Analyzer

Ensure your color choices meet accessibility standards. Our tool helps you check and improve the readability of your text against various background colors.

Font Legibility Tester

Experiment with different fonts and sizes to find the most readable combination. Improve the legibility of your content for all users, especially those with visual impairments.
